I'm thrilled to announce that florian-kromer.eu has been successfully migrated from Ghost Pro to Astro! This transition represents a significant step towards a more sustainable, privacy-focused web presence.

## Why the Migration?

After using Ghost Pro for some time, I decided to explore alternatives that better align with my values regarding data privacy and environmental sustainability. The migration to Astro offers several compelling advantages:

### Benefits of Astro

- **Static Site Generation**: Astro generates static HTML, reducing server load and energy consumption
- **Superior Performance**: Lightning-fast page loads with minimal JavaScript overhead
- **Developer Experience**: Modern tooling with a pleasant development workflow
- **Flexibility**: Easy integration with components and content management

## European, Data-Friendly Hosting

A crucial part of this migration was selecting a web hosting provider that respects data privacy and environmental concerns. I'm now hosted on a European infrastructure that prioritizes:

- **GDPR Compliance**: Full compliance with European data protection regulations
- **Data Sovereignty**: Your data stays within European data centers
- **Green Hosting**: Infrastructure powered by renewable energy sources
- **Privacy First**: No unnecessary data collection or third-party tracking

## What This Means for Visitors

By choosing an Astro-based static site with European hosting, this website now:

- Loads faster with minimal environmental impact
- Respects your privacy with no tracking or invasive analytics
- Complies with GDPR and European data protection standards
- Supports sustainable web practices

## Looking Forward

This migration marks the beginning of a more conscious approach to web development and hosting. I'm committed to maintaining this site with privacy, performance, and sustainability at the forefront.
